## 8. Experiments coverage (README ¬ß18)

### Experiment 1 ‚Äî Static graph comparison

Compare `manual_graph`, `fully_connected`, `random_sparse`, `flybrain_prior_untrained` (‚âà FlyBrain prior graph). All four ship in `flybrain.baselines` and are part of the `static` and `full_min` suites; `comparison_overall` rows above cover this.

### Experiment 2 ‚Äî Embedding ablation

Controller variants `learned_router_no_prior` (no embeddings) vs. `flybrain_prior_untrained` (task + agent + graph embeddings) are both in `full_min`; deeper ablations (task-only, task+agent, +trace, +graph) require running individual controller configs in `configs/eval/`.

### Experiment 3 ‚Äî Verification ablation

The verifier is on/off via `MASConfig.verification_mode`. The current run uses the full layer; toggling needs a config override ‚Äî track via a follow-up benchmark slice and re-run `flybrain-py report`.

### Experiment 4 ‚Äî Training ablation

`flybrain_prior_untrained` ‚Üí `flybrain_sim_pretrain` ‚Üí `flybrain_imitation` ‚Üí `flybrain_rl` is exactly the training progression; rows in section 2 above show the four points.

### Experiment 5 ‚Äî Full-benchmark generalization

Finetune on 10-20% (`min_set.yaml`) and evaluate on the full suite via `flybrain-py bench --suite full_min`; this run is the eval half of that experiment.
